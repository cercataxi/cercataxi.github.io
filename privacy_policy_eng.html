1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites you visit send to the user's terminal (usually the browser), where they are stored to be retransmitted to the same sites on the next visit. They are used to improve the Browse experience, for statistical analysis, or to display personalized advertising content.

2. Types of cookies used

a) Technical cookies (necessary)
These cookies are essential for the proper functioning of the website and the app. They allow, for example, the management of authentication, sessions, and security.

Examples: session cookies, user preference cookies.

b) Analytics cookies
Used to collect aggregated information about users' use of the site/app (number of visits, most viewed pages, time spent, etc.). They can be:

  • First-party: managed directly by CERCA.TAXI.
  • Third-party: such as Google Analytics (anonymized IP).

c) Profiling cookies (with prior consent)
Used to track user navigation and create profiles in order to send advertising messages in line with expressed preferences.

Ex. Facebook Pixel, Google Ads, behavioral advertising cookies.

3. Consent management

Upon first access, a banner is displayed allowing:

  • Acceptance of all cookies;
  • Rejection of non-technical cookies;
  • Customization of preferences.

Consent can be revoked at any time via [link or "Manage preferences" button in the footer].

4. How to disable cookies from your browser

The user can configure their browser to accept or reject all cookies, or only some (e.g., block third-party cookies). Links for major browsers:

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Edge

5. Third-party cookies

This site/app may include third-party functionalities (e.g., Google Maps, YouTube videos, social buttons), which may install their own cookies. Please refer to their respective privacy and cookie policies for more details.

8. Geolocation data processing (GPS)

The CERCA.TAXI app uses the taxi driver's geolocation data exclusively for operational purposes, namely:

  • Assignment and management of rides;
  • Real-time position display to the customer during the ride;
  • Optimization of matching between supply and demand.

Retention methods and times:

  • Position data is retained only for the time strictly necessary for the provision of the service in real-time.
  • Once the ride is concluded or the usage session ends, GPS data is automatically deleted from our systems, except for those that must be stored in aggregated and anonymous form for statistical or anti-fraud purposes.
  • No continuous geolocation is performed when the app is closed or the taxi driver is offline.

Sharing with third parties:

  • Location data is not transferred, communicated, or shared with third parties for commercial or profiling purposes.

8. Geolocation data processing (GPS and/or approximate position)

The use of geolocation by CERCA.TAXI complies with the principles of data minimization and storage limitation, as provided by the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). In particular:

  • Purpose of processing: geolocation data is used exclusively to identify the user's position in order to show available taxis nearby and facilitate the contact request with the taxi driver.
  • Legal basis: processing is based on the user's consent, expressed through the authorization to use location in the browser or device.
  • Processing methods: position data is used in real-time and is not stored or saved in CERCA.TAXI systems. Once the session ends or the page/app is closed, all information is automatically deleted.

  • Data subject rights: the user has the right to revoke consent for geolocation at any time, directly from the browser or device settings.

These practices are in line with the guidelines provided by the Data Protection Authority, which emphasize the importance of processing geolocation data in a proportionate manner and limited to the specific purposes of the service.

  • Continue using the site with limited functionalities (e.g., manual position entry).
